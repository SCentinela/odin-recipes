Lasagna

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish that consists of stacked layers of flat pasta, typically alternating with fillings like ground meat, vegetables, cheese, and sauce.

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • For the Bolognese Sauce:
    • 500 g (1 lb) ground beef (you can mix with pork if you like)
    • 1 large onion, finely chopped
    • 2 garlic cloves, minced
    • 2 medium carrots, peeled and finely chopped
    • 1 can (400 g / 14 oz) crushed tomatoes
    • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
    • 1 cup beef broth or water
    • 2 bay leaves
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Olive oil

    • Nutmeg to taste
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Assembly:
    • 12 lasagna sheets (you can use fresh or dried)
    • 250 g (9 oz) ricotta cheese
    • 200 g (7 oz) mozzarella cheese, grated
    • 100 g (3 1/2 oz) Parmesan cheese, grated
